I am a PhD Candidate in the MAC-MIGS CDT at the Maxwell Institute for Mathematical science. My work is at the intersection of stochastic processes, optimization, sampling and computational statistics with applications to machine learning. My PhD supervisors are Dr. Paul Dobson from Heriot Watt University and Prof. Konstantinos Zygalakis from The University of Edinburgh.

Prior to my PhD studies, I completed a structured Master at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS), Cameroon, with a focus on PDEs, and an MSc at Université Paris Dauphine, where I worked on optimal control of PDEs.
